Bio

Who I'm? Where I'm from? I'm a famous artist? Did I win some prizes?

These are all the question people ask when you say that you are an artist.

But all these really matters?

What I can say is that I love art, because art is everywhere around us.

The most famous artist in the universe is probably the Nature.

Nature is so perfect and imperfect at the same time.

Nature can show you the perfection through flower, rocks and all the geometrical perfect forms you can find in nature.

Nature can also be imperfect if you just watch its work in sunset or sunrise.

But everything is completly emotional, leaving you astonished just watching its creation.

For these reasons I like photography, it allow you to capture every single perfection and imperfection of nature. So I'm a photographer.

But what is better than taking a brusk and try to do something beautiful yourself? I'm also a painter.

And what about the perfection of nature? I like to take the ruler and draw something symmetrically perfect. I'm also a technical designer. 

I'm also a millenials. This means that technology is a part of me. Well, I can say that I'm a digital artist, because I also like to create art with softwares.

This is all you need to know about me. Nothing about my name, my age, my birthplace.

Maybe you need to know that I'm also a musician. Because art is not only visual.

The other thing you need to know is my name stage: breaking symmetry.
